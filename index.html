Services we offer

Skimming


Skimming is a process used to create a smooth and even surface on your walls and ceilings. It involves applying a thin layer of plaster over existing surfaces to improve their appearance.

Rendering


Rendering is the application of a mixture of sand, cement, and water to exterior walls, creating a durable finish that enhances the aesthetic appeal and weather resistance of your home.

Plastering


Plastering involves applying a layer of plaster to walls and ceilings to create a flat, smooth surface ready for painting or wallpapering, enhancing both beauty and durability.
